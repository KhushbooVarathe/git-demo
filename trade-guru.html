Trade Guru Trading Strategies

Trend Following: Trend-following tactics, which entail spotting and pursuing a market trend, are highly recommended by trading gurus.

Risk Management: Experts in trading frequently stress how crucial risk management is to successful trading. This can involve controlling position sizes to reduce risk exposure..

Market Analysis: Gurus typically rely on a combination of fundamental and technical analysis to make trading decisions.

Algorithmic Trading: Many trade gurus use algorithmic trading systems to execute their trades.

Why Partner With HFT?

Hands-Free Trading simplifies the management of trading strategies and automates trade order submission to your brokerage accounts. Hands-Free Trading provides a marketplace where trading strategy gurus can list their trading strategies. Customers browse this marketplace, subscribe to trading strategies, and seamlessly connect their brokerage accounts to start automated trading.

Our mission is to democratize access to automated trading, thus our company strives to empower customers to take a “Hands-Free approach” to trading.
